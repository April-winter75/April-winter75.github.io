5 Easy Sewing Ideas for Beginners

Read time: 10 minutes

Sewing is an amazing skill to develop...

So keep reading to find out more

This article is for you if you're thinking of starting a new sewing project but don't want something too difficult to finish.

"You can't use up creativity. The more you use, the more you have" - Maya Angelo

A Simple Skirt

It's essential you find a 'simple' skirt to make, because you can easily get an over complicated pattern that has a bunch of elements and pieces causing you to have an unnecessarily difficult experience. Therefore I recommend you find a simple A-line pattern and once the skill is further developed you can be brave and choose a pleated skirt.

A Tote Bag

It's super easy and doesn't even need a pattern since it's only comprised of two sides and a pair of handles. Plus not much fabric is needed for each one, so you can make loads and sell them or have multiple colours/designs for each occasion.

A T-Shirt

A really quick item of clothing that is definitely an essential in your wardrobe. You can use different fabric and designs to make it fancy but a simple t-shirt will never be a hard thing to make and it's great practice to eventually make more complicated and intricate shirts.

A Blouse

This can vary in difficulty as they can be pretty straightforward or they can be draped and pleated with more complicated elements. However, it's recommended to choose a simple design and what makes a blouse the perfect choice is because of how versatile it can be.

Pyjamas

There is a huge variety in the styles of pyjamas you can sew and you can be very creative with crazy designs and embroidery. Although, the best thing about pyjamas is that it's okay to make a mistake especially if it's your first time sewing a garment because not many people will notice as pyjamas stay at home.

There are many other easy garments to make and the most important part to remember is that it won't be perfect and it takes time to get there.

Happy Sewing!
